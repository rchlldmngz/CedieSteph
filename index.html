Our Love Story


Cedrick had lived an extremely simple life, being studious and doing irrational things. He likes riddles, puzzles, and board games and prefers his own quiet space, surrounded only by some of his closest friends and family. On the contrary, Steph is busy exploring the vastness of the world and experiencing the finer things that life has to offer. She was very active in doing co-curricular and extracurricular activities in school and in her community, respectively, while maintaining her scholarship, while Cedrick, on the other hand, is determined to study harder every time. Steph was raised by her family to be loving and caring. This shows that love has no boundaries and is limitless, so that even opposites attract, somewhat and somehow.

One day, their worlds collided naturally but serendipitously on one ordinary weekday afternoon at the university’s engineering building. Cedrick was amazed by her and thought to himself that this could be something, and he was head over heels like never before. Just “one gaze,” and there are infinite possibilities when it comes to love. After months of getting to know each other, they became close friends. Coincidentally, they found out that Cedrick’s eldest brother was a high school classmate of Steph’s elder brother. Indeed, it is a small world. Slowly, they got to a place where they could completely be themselves together, striking balance with one another in all the right ways. They always aim to be indispensable to each other and to be successful together.

Finally, after having the confidence that he can give her the life she deserves, he asks the sweetest, “Will You Marry Me?" on a romantic date. She replied after seeing the photographers, “Wait! Let me use the washroom!” Kidding aside, it was actually because of a long traffic jam going to the restaurant that day, hence, at least an hour late for the dinner reservation. It was a fun night full of genuine love, great food, and laughter which was shared virtually with our loved ones. After more than a decade of love, Cedrick and Steph are getting married in front of their nearest and dearest loved ones, and most of all, with the blessing of God.

We are so grateful to each and every single person who showed their endless love and support, as well as for the lessons we've learned and the growth we've experienced together. We are excited to see you on our wedding day, as we say #SteffiCedIDo on July 26, 2024!

- John Cedrick & Stephanie Joy
